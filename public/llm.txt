# Resume Unleashed - AI-Powered Resume Builder

## Overview
Resume Unleashed is the ultimate **resume maker AI** and **CV maker AI** platform. As a comprehensive **AI resume builder free** to try, it helps job seekers create professional **artificial intelligence resumes**, ensures **ATS optimisation**, and manages their professional brand.

## Core Features

### Resume Management
- **AI Resume Creator**: Automatically tailor resumes to match specific job descriptions using the **resume generator AI**
- **ATS Optimisation**: Ensure resumes pass Applicant Tracking Systems with advanced **resume AI** analysis
- **Multiple Templates**: Professional resume templates optimized for **artificial intelligence resume** scanning
- **Resume Parser**: Upload and parse existing resumes
- **Version Control**: Track different versions of customized resumes

### Career Tools
- **AI Interview Prep**: Practice with realistic **interview with ai** simulations based on job descriptions
- **AI Interview**: Get real-time feedback on your responses
- **Salary Estimation**: Get salary insights for different roles and locations
- **Job Matching**: Match your skills with job requirements

### Professional Branding
- **Digital Visiting Cards**: Create professional digital business cards
- **Public Resume Links**: Share resumes via unique URLs with QR codes
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track resume views and engagement

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 16 (App Router), React, TypeScript
- **Styling**: TailwindCSS, shadcn/ui components
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage)
- **AI**: OpenAI API for resume customization and analysis
- **Payments**: Razorpay for subscription management
- **Analytics**: Vercel Analytics, Google Analytics

## Database Schema

### Tables
1. **profiles**: User profiles with role and company_id for RLS
2. **resumes**: Source resume data and metadata
3. **job_descriptions**: Job postings for AI matching
4. **resume_templates**: Resume and card templates
5. **customized_resumes**: AI-generated resume versions
6. **visiting_cards**: Digital business card data
7. **public_resume_links**: Shareable resume links with QR codes
8. **link_analytics**: View tracking and engagement metrics
9. **subscriptions**: User subscription and payment data

### Security
- Row Level Security (RLS) enabled on all tables
- JWT metadata includes role and company_id
- Authentication via Supabase Auth (email/password, OAuth)
